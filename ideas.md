## Selected Design: **Obsidian Luxury Athleticism** (Response 3)

This approach best captures SnugLegX's brand identity — premium fitness apparel that merges athletic performance with luxury aesthetics. The obsidian + champagne gold palette signals exclusivity while the brand's heart-infinity logo becomes a living, breathing centerpiece. The typographic contrast between Bebas Neue (power) and Cormorant Garamond (elegance) mirrors the brand's dual identity.
